Introduction

Welcome to the introduction of my interaction design, focused on gamifying a learning app. our aim is to make the learning experience more engaging and interactive for our users.

Gamification involves applying game design principles to non-game context, such as education. the objective is to increase user motivation, engagement, and enjoyment.

This project involves various phases, including a competitive analysis, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. The competitive analysis will help me indentify strenghts and weaknesses in the existing market, and hightlight areas we can improve.

Wireframing and prototyping allowed us to disign and develop the interface and user experience of the app, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable learning process.

Finally, usability testing was conducted to gather feedback from users on the app's usability and functionality. The feedback will be used to rifine and improve the app, ensuring that the user's needs are met.

Overall, our aim is to create a learning app that is not only effective but also enjoyble, ultimatly improving the user's learning outcomes.
